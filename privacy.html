Privacy Policy



1. Introduction

Welcome to our Project Management Platform Privacy Policy. We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a safe and secure user experience.

2. Information We Collect

We collect both personally identifiable and non-personally identifiable information from users. This includes:

  • Personal information (name, email, contact details) provided during account registration and profile setup.
  • Project-related information such as project names, descriptions, and documents uploaded.
  • Usage data, including log files, IP addresses, browser type, and pages visited.
3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for various purposes, including:

  • Providing and improving our project management services.
  • Communicating with you about your account, projects, and platform updates.
  • Customizing your experience and preferences.
  • Analyzing usage patterns and trends to enhance our platform.
4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption, firewalls, and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Disclosure of Information

We may share your information with third parties only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent.
  • To comply with legal obligations or protect our rights and interests.
  • With service providers who assist in delivering our services.
6. Your Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access, modify, or delete your personal information.
  • Opt-out of receiving promotional emails.
  • Close your account at any time.
7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the date of the last update will be indicated.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy, please contact our support team at support@managlo.com.
